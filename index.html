Luxury Pool Experiences
Swimming Pools

Villa Kiin Ha boasts three stunning pools, each designed to cater to different needs. The main pool is spacious, surrounded by loungers and lush greenery, making it perfect for families to relax and enjoy. The swim-up pool, connecting directly to the bedrooms, is the standout feature of the property, offering unparalleled convenience and luxury. Additionally, the master bedroom features its own private pool for exclusive, serene enjoyment.

  • Main pool with loungers and family-friendly space

  • Top-notch swim-up pool with direct room access

  • Private pool exclusive to the master bedroom
